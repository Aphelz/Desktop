    About Us

    In the Western Cape and Gauteng, HairOnTap is one of the most outstanding brands in the hair and beauty care industry, giving hairstyling a new skyline. HairOnTap, which is quickly growing to be the largest single salon chain in the country, is also widely present in Kwazulu Natal, Durban.

    With more than 30 employees, the company ensures that everyone's skills are sharpened at least once a year. Looks has developed throughout time, adopting new techniques and trends while keeping its beauticians up to date with rapidly changing trends in the domestic and international market. By enrolling the beauticians in various classes and encouraging them to participate in a variety of events of all kinds, HairOnTap consistently aims to educate and prepare their beauticians on the most modern techniques and styles with cutting-edge innovation.
